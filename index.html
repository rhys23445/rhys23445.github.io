About Us

1

First Started

Iron dancers was originally created on the 30th july 2021. We were formally know as The Bees Crew and decided to rebrand to the Iron Dancers that we now know today!

2

Our Theme

Our theme is obviously the knights of shining armour but we also added a little bit of dance into it! Our main colour is grey with black being our secondary colour

3

Dressage

Iron Dancers follows the famous Star Stables Dressage dictionary! We like to dominate the arena while performing the SSD moves and move together in harmony. Events are at 7:30pm till 8:30pm British time!

4

Our aims

The main aim for Iron Dancers is to provide a safe and positive enviroment for the members. We will tollerate no bullying by anyone within the club and outside the club!
